Muelle Viejo

Experience the natural beauty of Coveñas with our Ruta Muelle Viejo tour. This scenic 3.5-kilometer paddle near the beaches of Coveñas takes you to the remains of the old Copetrol dock. The dock is always populated by pelicans, seagulls, and other birds, making it a perfect spot for birdwatching enthusiasts.
